# MVP Scope and Success Metrics

## MVP Objective
Deliver a coherent end-to-end workflow enabling business teams and agencies to generate branded slide decks via conversational AI, with minimal manual formatting.

## Core Capabilities
1. **Account & Brand Setup**
   - Workspace creation, SSO/OAuth login.
   - Brand kit upload (logo, colors, fonts, tone descriptors).
2. **Content Intake Wizard**
   - Structured form for topic, audience, objectives, sources.
   - File/URL ingestion (PDF, DOCX, XLSX) with text extraction and summarization.
3. **Conversational Outline Planning**
   - Chat interface to negotiate deck structure, with AI-proposed sections and rationale.
   - Ability to accept, edit, or request alternatives per section.
4. **Slide Draft Generation**
   - Automated creation of headlines, bullets, speaker notes aligned to brand tone.
   - Per-slide quick actions: rewrite, shorten, re-tone, regenerate visuals.
5. **Deck Rendering & Export**
   - Generate editable PPTX and Google Slides versions, plus PDF snapshot.
   - Maintain brand styling consistency across slides.
6. **Collaboration Basics**
   - Commenting/mentioning within chat, version history of iterations.
7. **Analytics Baseline**
   - Track time saved per deck, AI usage metrics, basic engagement events.

## Out-of-Scope for MVP
- Advanced analytics dashboards or engagement tracking post-delivery.
- Deep integrations with CRM/BI beyond file uploads.
- Multi-language translation and voiceover generation.
- Offline desktop editor.

## Success Metrics
- **Activation:** ≥70% of onboarded teams complete deck generation within first week.
- **Efficiency:** Median time from intake to export ≤15 minutes for 10-slide deck.
- **Quality:** ≥80% of users rate generated decks “ready with minor edits” or better in post-session survey.
- **Retention:** 40% of workspaces create ≥3 decks within first month.
- **Reliability:** 99% successful export rate across PPTX/Google Slides/PDF.

## Validation Plan
- Conduct closed beta with 5 internal teams + 3 agency partners.
- Measure NPS and qualitative feedback after each deck session.
- Iterate on prompt templates and UI flows based on observed friction.
