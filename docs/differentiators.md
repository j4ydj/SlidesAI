# Differentiators and Growth Backlog

## MVP-Adjacent Differentiators
- **Brand Governance Mode:** Automated checks for logo usage, color contrast, and tone compliance, providing instant fix suggestions.
- **Narrative Intelligence:** Conversation agent summarizes rationale for each section, aiding stakeholder alignment.
- **Reusable Frameworks:** Save and apply successful deck structures (e.g., quarterly business review, campaign recap) across accounts.
- **Insight Capture:** Auto-generate executive summaries and action lists from the final deck for quick distribution.

## Near-Term Enhancements (Post-MVP)
- **Engagement Analytics:** Track slide-level viewing and feedback metrics when decks are shared, surfacing which sections resonate.
- **Live Data Feeds:** Integrate with BI dashboards to auto-refresh charts and tables before meetings.
- **Client Collaboration Portal:** Agency clients review/comment in a branded portal with approval workflows.
- **Voice & Language Adaptation:** Offer multilingual copy generation and voice persona presets for global teams.

## Long-Term Bets
- **Generative Visuals:** Integrate AI image/video generation tuned to brand styles for bespoke visuals.
- **Interactive Presentations:** Produce web-native experiences with embedded data stories, not just static slides.
- **API & Automation Marketplace:** Allow partners to build custom automations (e.g., CRM-triggered pitch decks).
- **Predictive Deck Coaching:** Analyze historic performance data to recommend narrative tweaks likely to convert.

## Competitive Guardrails
- Continually benchmark against GenPPT and SlidesAI feature releases to maintain parity on core slide generation promises.
- Emphasize measurable time savings and quality versus "slide generators" that only provide templates.
- Leverage agency case studies highlighting collaboration tooling as a differentiator.
