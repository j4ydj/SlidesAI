# Personas and Jobs to Be Done

## Overview
This document summarizes target user personas for the AI Slide Copilot product, focusing on internal business teams and agencies/consultancies. It captures key goals, pain points, workflows, and critical jobs to be done.

## Persona 1: Business Insights Manager (Internal Team)
- **Context:** Works within a mid-sized enterprise, responsible for recurring performance reports, quarterly business reviews, and executive briefings.
- **Goals:** Deliver data-backed narratives quickly, maintain brand fidelity, adapt tone for executive vs. operational audiences.
- **Pain Points:** Manual slide assembly from spreadsheets/docs, repetitive formatting tasks, tight turnaround for leadership updates, lack of collaboration visibility.
- **Jobs to Be Done:**
  - Transform data sources (BI exports, docs) into presentation-ready narratives.
  - Verify messaging aligns with corporate tone and compliance rules.
  - Iterate collaboratively with stakeholders in a conversational workflow.
  - Export decks to PowerPoint/Google Slides with minimal cleanup required.

## Persona 2: Agency Account Strategist
- **Context:** Works at a marketing/creative agency; produces client pitches, campaign recaps, and proposals across multiple brands.
- **Goals:** Generate polished decks that reflect each clientâ€™s brand, reuse best-performing structures, respond quickly to client feedback.
- **Pain Points:** Switching between brand guidelines, version chaos, difficulty scaling deck production across accounts, time-consuming design tweaks.
- **Jobs to Be Done:**
  - Ingest briefs, discovery notes, and external assets to generate deck outlines.
  - Apply brand kits (fonts, colors, tone) across slides automatically.
  - Co-create with clients via chat, capturing revisions and rationale.
  - Produce deliverables in PPTX/PDF while preserving editable elements.

## Shared Requirements
- Support for structured intake (objectives, audience, key messages).
- LLM assistance that explains reasoning, proposes alternatives, and adapts to feedback.
- Automated slide design with per-slide refresh/rewrite controls.
- Centralized asset management (logo sets, charts, media) and permissions.
- Analytics or activity logs for accountability and optimization.
